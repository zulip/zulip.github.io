Contribute to the open source project

We'd love your help

Zulip is licensed under the Apache license and is a friendly open source community that welcomes all forms of contributions! There's a ton of stuff to do -- in development, testing, translation, documentation, and more. Here's just a small sampling of ways you can get started:

  • Write an integration for your favorite service
  • Improve our Dockerfile to make Zulip available to dockerized servers
  • Translate user-visible words into another language
  • Build an efficient process for testing and releasing new versions of the desktop apps
  • Update our screenshots to show the current Zulip UI

And here's our roadmap, so you can see where we're headed in 2016.

Get started

Learn how to set up a Zulip server development environment!

We've written developer documentation, available on Read The Docs, that covers how to write a new feature, how to translate Zulip, our "Bugdown" flavor of Markdown, and more.

Zulip also has a development discussion mailing list (plus lists for iOS dev and Android dev). Please reach out to us if you have any questions!
